Berdan's Best Movies

My top three movies of all time.

Tron Legacy (2011)

One of the most un-Disney Disney movies of all time. A great tale about family, mistakes, perfection, fascism and redemption. Oh, it's also in a supercomputer too.

Star Wars Episode III:Revenge of The Sith (2005)

An epic hero's downfall story set in a galaxy that is torn apart by a long and destructive civil war, wrapped within the story of how a republic can turn into a monarchy. It also has lightsabers.

Dune (2021) and Dune:Part Two (2023)

I know, those are two movies but considering the story they tell, seeing them as one long movie makes a lot of sense. It's a lengthy but magnificent story about freedom, war, love, lineage and religious propaganda. It is a blueprint of how idolization of persons can lead people to their downfall.
